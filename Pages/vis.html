A little bit about the visualisation

I liked the above data visualisaton since usually these types of trees do not have a time wise aspect that relates to real world time but rather they refer to series of events. The standard tree view is one of a cladogram (or dendrogram) produced by a hierarchical clustering algorithm yet this one is quite creative with the way it presents this cladogram ontop of a swirling track. One critique would be that in order to get the lower time wise elements of the tree they use organism sequences from recent organisms and not the ones of the actual ancestor since we do not have that one (though a prediction could be made), which means that we see relation plotted on a time axis.
